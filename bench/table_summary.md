## Bound analysis

At D=30, best observed achieved bandwidth was 203.7 GB/s
(30.3% of 672 GB/s peak) and best observed
achieved compute was 144.8 GFLOPS (0.9% of
16312 GFLOPS peak).

BW utilization dominates FLOPS utilization — the kernel is **memory-bound** at these sizes.
